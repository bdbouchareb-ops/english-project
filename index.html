About ENSTA

The National Higher School of Advanced Technologies (ENSTA) : is a prestigious Algerian institution that combines innovation, engineering, and scientific research in cutting-edge technological fields. Founded in 2022 through the merger of two renowned schools, ENSTA trains highly qualified engineers and researchers ready to shape the future of Algeria’s technological development. The school offers two main fields of study: Science and Technology, covering specializations such as electronics, mechanics, automation, and industrial engineering. Computer Science, including programs in artificial intelligence and cyber security. As a national center of excellence, ENSTA promotes creativity, entrepreneurship, and applied research to meet the needs of the modern economy and drive innovation across industries.

Math and Informatics

Artificial Intelligence

The Artificial Intelligence program focuses on designing intelligent systems, covering machine learning, neural networks, data analysis, and AI applications to solve real-world problems.

System Security

The System Security program trains students to protect information systems from threats, covering cryptography, network security, secure programming, and defense strategies. Graduates can design and maintain secure systems for both public and private sectors.
